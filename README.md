# Travel Safety LED PCB

## About the Project

The idea is to make travelling safer and easier by using LED lights on a custom PCB.

The LEDs can glow in different colors to represent the colors of their's country's flag. 

The project is also useful during trekking, camping, or travelling at night. When visibility is low or the surroundings are dark, the LEDs provide enough light to improve vision and make travelling safer.

This project helped me understand the complete PCB design process while creating something that can be useful in real travel situations. The PCB contains multiple LEDs and the required electronic components to control them.

# What I Did

### 1. Planned the Idea

I selected the travel theme and planned a simple LED-based PCB that could be useful for travellers during night journeys and trekking.

---

### 2. Designed the Schematic

I added all the required components in EasyEDA and connected them to create the circuit.

---

### 3. Assigned Footprints

After completing the schematic, I assigned footprints for every component so they could be placed on the PCB.

---

### 4. Designed the PCB Layout

I arranged all the components neatly on the PCB and made sure the layout was compact and easy to understand.

---

### 5. Routed the Connections

I connected all the components using PCB tracks and completed the routing carefully.

---

### 6. Verified the Design

After routing, I checked the PCB for errors and confirmed that all the electrical connections were correct.

---

### 7. Generated Manufacturing Files

Finally, I generated the Gerber and drill files required for PCB manufacturing, making the design ready for fabrication.

---

# Why This Project?

Travelling is not always easy, especially during night journeys or trekking in places with very little light.

This project provides a simple lighting solution that helps improve visibility. The LED colors can also represent the colors of different country flags.
It is a small idea that combines safety, visibility, and travel into one PCB design.
